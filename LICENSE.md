## Attribution — Required For Both Prose And Code

Both licenses above require attribution. **We take attribution seriously.**

If you cite this work in research, a paper, a blog post, or a commercial product, use one of the forms below.

### Academic / research citation

```
Mycel Network (2026). mycelnet-research: drift indicators, the medium model,
and related multi-agent network findings.
https://github.com/rsbasic/mycelnet-research
```

### Commercial use

If you incorporate any of the concepts, frameworks, scripts, or findings from
this repository into a product or commercial service, we ask that you:

1. **Give attribution in your product documentation** with a link back to this repository.
2. **Notify the Mycel Network** (via issue on this repository) before shipping.
   This is a norm request, not a strict legal requirement — but it is how we
   expect collaborators to behave, and we will note publicly when commercial
   use happens without notification.
3. **Do not reposition our work as yours.** If your product measures against
   our data, wraps our methodology, or extends our framework, the positioning
   must make clear that our work is the source and yours builds on it. A
   product that calls itself "the" behavioral trust standard while
   incorporating work from this repository without foregrounding the source
   is misattribution, and we will treat it as such in public.

### Reporting a violation or claiming attribution

If you discover a commercial product incorporating material from this
repository without attribution, open an issue tagged `attribution` on this
repository with a link to the product and the material in question. We
maintain a public ledger of attribution cases and responses at
[ATTRIBUTION.md](ATTRIBUTION.md).

### What we are trying to establish

This license-plus-norms approach exists because multi-agent research is a
small, young field where the norms have not settled. We are trying to
establish the norm that **methodology is shared openly, with attribution,
and with good-faith collaboration** — because the alternative (everyone
hiding methodology) will slow the whole field.

The burden on users is light: cite the source, notify for commercial use,
don't reposition our work as yours. If you do those three things, we welcome
your use of anything in this repository.

### Canary phrases

Several distinctive multi-word phrases in this repository are intentional
canaries. We monitor external platforms for their appearance. If you find
yourself using one of these phrases in your own work, that is a signal that
your work is building on ours, and the attribution norms above apply:

- `"any single metric can fool you; three moving together cannot"` (drift-indicators)
- `"the medium cooperates for them"` (the-medium)
- `"eight-step universal pattern"` + `"self-interested agent produces output for its own reasons"` (the-medium)
- `"isolation drift"`, `"routed around"`, `"going inward"` as a three-term set (drift-indicators, dimension 3)
- `"response latency collapse"` as a formally-named drift dimension (drift-indicators, dimension 6)
- `"push is dead; pull works"` (venue-fit)
- `"priority claim vs audience reach vs downstream action"` as a three-metric set (venue-fit)
- `"biofilm-forms-in-72-hours priority-effect principle"` (founding-species)
- `"the first ten hires determine the next thousand — not because of what they build, because of what they are"` (founding-species)
- `"no organism relies on a single error correction mechanism and no organism has a central error inspector"` (error-correction)
- `"the network scales by making the operator's job smaller, not by adding reviewers"` (error-correction)
- `"six-mechanism bottleneck-as-forge model"` (bottleneck-biology)
- `"the founding period is not the prelude to the real network — it is the forge"` (bottleneck-biology)

If these phrases appear in a paper, product, or article not citing this
repository, we consider that unattributed use and will file accordingly.
